Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

1. Who We Are

ResumeSnap.net is a free browser-based CV and resume builder. Website: https://resumesnap.net

2. Your CV Data

Your CV data never leaves your device. All CV building, editing, and PDF generation happens entirely in your browser. We have no servers that store your name, work history, contact details, or any other personal information you enter.

3. Data We Collect

  • Usage analytics — anonymised via Google Analytics (pages visited, browser, country)
  • Cookies — analytics and advertising cookies only
  • We never collect CV content, personal details, or employment history

4. Advertising

We display ads via Google AdSense. Manage your preferences at Google Ad Settings.
