What is ATS?

ATS stands for Applicant Tracking System — software used by most large employers to screen CVs before a human ever sees them. Our templates are designed to pass ATS scans by using clean formatting, standard section headings, and readable fonts. This significantly increases your chances of getting your CV in front of a real recruiter.
